Usability Verification for the Hyfe CoughMonitor Suite (CMS)

  • Feedback from 25 real-world users shows the Hyfe CoughMonitor Suite (CMS) is highly usable for adults with chronic cough or related respiratory conditions.
  • 96% of participants found the watch easy to use, all were able to wear and operate it correctly, and the average usability rating was 8.6/10.
  • No serious use-related hazards or misuse patterns were identified; participants consistently used the device safely and as intended.
  • Minor issues were noted, including the watch feeling large on smaller wrists and occasional confusion about dashboard visibility or data interpretation.
  • Users appreciated minimal setup, long battery life, simple charging, and effortless day-to-day operation.
  • Several users requested clearer access to personal cough data and more intuitive dashboard explanations.
